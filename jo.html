Jo March

Embark on a journey of self-discovery and reflection with Jo March, the passionate writer from Louisa May Alcott’s Little Women. This website offers 30 journaling prompts designed to inspire and challenge your inner writer. Just like Jo, who poured her thoughts and dreams onto paper, you too can find solace and creativity in journaling.

Our prompts are divided into several categories:

Anaphoric Sets: Use a specific set of words to create a powerful and cohesive narrative.
Questions: Thought-provoking questions that encourage deep reflection about your life, dreams, and experiences.
Letters: Write heartfelt letters to someone special, whether it's a loved one, a historical figure, or even your future self.
Dear X Person: Address your thoughts and feelings to a particular person, offering a unique perspective on your relationships and emotions.

Each prompt is crafted to help you explore your thoughts, reflect on your day, and understand yourself better. Obviously, you can adapt them according to your interests and let Jo March’s spirit guide you through your writing journey.
